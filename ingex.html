Main Panda

Deep in the bamboo forests of China lived two pandas , Yong-Hu and Ho-Shing. Yong-Hu was rather plump, had black ears, legs and arms, and two large black patches around his golden eyes. Ho-Shing looked much the same but wasn’t as chubby as Yong-Hu. Yong-Hu spent most of his days sitting among the bamboo eating.
